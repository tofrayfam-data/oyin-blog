5 Parenting Mistakes You Don’t Realize

Daily words and actions affect children deeply. Small habits can shape their future in ways parents often don’t notice. Awareness is the first step to raising confident, God-fearing children.

1. Comparing Your Child to Others

Saying things like “Why can’t you be more like your sibling?” or “Other kids are better at this” can harm self-esteem.

  • Focus on your child’s strengths
  • Celebrate progress, not perfection
  • Encourage effort over comparison
