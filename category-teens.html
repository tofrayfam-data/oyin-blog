How to Talk So Kids Listen

Children listen better when they feel respected and heard. Communication is not just about giving instructions — it’s about connecting.

1. Listen Actively

Before giving advice or corrections, really listen to your child. Put down distractions, maintain eye contact, and acknowledge their feelings.

Tips for active listening:

  • Paraphrase what they say: “I hear that you’re upset because…”
  • Ask open-ended questions: “How did that make you feel?”
  • Validate feelings without judgment
